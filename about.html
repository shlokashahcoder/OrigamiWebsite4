About Origami



Origami is art of paper-folding. Its name derives from words ori("folding") and kami("paper")


Traditional origami consists of folding a single sheet of square paper into sculpture without cutting, gluing, taping or even marking it.


Origami is a mind relexing art and makes one feel happy. It is also very creative
